Asia TEEP Intern 2019

NTUST Intern

Technologies Used: Thingworx, Postman
Languages used: Python 3, CPP
Deployed at: NTUST BMW Lab


I was given this opportunity to visit the country of Taiwan in the summer of 2019, where I had got an internship as ASIA Teep intern 2019 in the BMW Lab at the prestigious institute of NTUST. The work for my internship there was to make a working simulator for a smart water dispenser using CPP and python for parameter derivation. The project was successfully completed by me and here are some screenshots of the project GUI.
For further details and codes of the project please refer to the link Taiwan Internship

I had bagged the internship in my third year after reaching out to Prof. Ray Gueng Cheng via mail. I had obtained his mail id from ASIA Teep intern website where the projects and the colleges were available. After getting in touch with him he had given me a series of tasks initially to help me know more on the project and also decide on my internship. You can know more about my experience in the internship here.
