Qualcomm

Qnterns

I was given the opportunity to join Qualcomm during the summer of 2020 as a summer intern, working with the team of IoT security my primary task was to validate video playback on a HDMI screen being passed from a secure connection using Deep Learning Techniques. In total I had created an overall of 7 modules with respective call APIs for the video playback validation. Video playback validation revolves around the fact that if or not the correct requested video is being displayed on the screen.

I had been selected after an Online Test which mostly comprised of coding questions and an aptitude round. This round was followed by a telephonic interview round in which I was majorly asked questions related to Machine Learning and Deep Learning and explain in detail about the projects that I have implemented throughout my career. Later, I received a mail confirming my internship results.

This year being different from others we were offered to complete the internships in a work from home scenario. It was great as the whole team was equally supportive as in a face to face scenario. With onboarding of more than 200 interns it was never any doubt left unsolved. This was a great opportunity for me to learn and grow at the same time.
