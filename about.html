About Ankit Kumar Panda

The Introduction

Hello! I am Ankit Kumar Panda an undergraduate student currently pursuing his dual degree at NIT Rourkela. I am currently in my pre-final year of study in Electronics and Communication Engineering. I am highly passionate about Data Science and have successully implemented projects and gained experience via internships in the sector also.

It all started with me working on Internet of Things with sensors where I was obtaining data and storing them in databases. This got me thinking why not do some analysis with the data and thus began my journey into data science. I have been involved in developing solutions for many complex problem statements wherein Data Science could be used to simply automate the work and help making lives easier.

Through my data analysis skills and analytical reasoning, I have always wanted to see my solutions being deployed and used for the better of the world that we live in. Other than data science, I am also proficient in Data Structures and Algorithms which are an inherent part of Data Science.
